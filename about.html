Our Story

Where It All Began

Smooth.IT was founded in 2020 by three friends who were frustrated with the beauty industry. Products were either too heavy, too expensive, or full of ingredients that irritated their skin. They wanted something different — something clean, effective, and genuinely enjoyable to use.

Starting from a small lab in Los Angeles, they developed their first formula: the now-iconic Blurring Primer. The response was extraordinary. Word spread, the community grew, and Smooth.IT became the brand that thousands of beauty lovers trust every single day.

Today, we offer 12 hero products spanning skin prep, color, and tools — all formulated with clean, skin-loving ingredients and tested by real people with real skin. Our mission hasn't changed: makeup that makes you feel effortlessly, beautifully yourself.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Three pillars that guide everything we create.

🌸

Clean Beauty

Every formula is free from parabens, sulfates, synthetic fragrance, and harmful fillers. We only use ingredients we'd put on our own skin — because we do.

✨

Inclusive Shade Ranges

Beauty is for everyone. We formulate with the full spectrum of skin tones in mind, from the fairest porcelain to the deepest ebony — and everything in between.

💛

Sustainable Practices

Our packaging is fully recyclable, our lab runs on renewable energy, and we're committed to reducing our carbon footprint by 50% by 2027. Beauty with a conscience.
